Compose Window - Thunderbird 38 Goals
Thunderbird 31 implemented many smaller (but significant) features to the compose window. Smarter attachment reminders and improved look/feel. The next major version (38) will attempt to implement more significant features in an effort to improve one of the most important (and often over-looked) areas of email.

Feature Changes
